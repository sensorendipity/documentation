settings

For this app to work, you will need: 1. Your phone/devices and computer have to be connected with the same WiFi network. 1. Your android device has to be version 4.0 or better.

Steps to install the app on your phone:

1. (Optional if you have File Explorer on your phone) Download ES File Explorer on your device: http://goo.gl/jQBhs 1. Enable third party app installation on your Android device 1. Access your phone’s Settings 1. Go to Security 1. Check the box beside ‘Unknown Sources’ 1. Download BasicSense-.apk [link] into your phone 1. Connect your android phone to computer, and transfer the BasicSense-.apk to your phone storage 1. Find BasicSense-.apk on your phone with File Explorer. 1. Click BasicSense-.apk 1. Click Install 1. If you see this screen, the installation is completed!

Remember! Your phone and computer must be connected with the same WiFi network and turned on GPS before you use Sensorendipity.
