FAQs

  1. What is the level of knowledge required to build a web app using Sensorendipity? Relax. just basic knowledge of JS and HTML.
  2. Can I use my iOS or any other Device ? Sorry, but currently this is only available for Android devices.
  3. Do I have to pay anything to use Sensorendipity ? Not at all, we believe in making the learning and building.
  4. Can I make changes in the apk file ? No, but soon we are planning to make the code open-source.
  5. Can I reuse the examples and build something upon them ? Yes, collaboration builds a richer understanding
  6. How do I report a bug or contact Sensorendipity team ? Please send us an email at lu.weiquan@nus.edu.sg
